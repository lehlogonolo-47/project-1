About Us

Driving South Africa's Energy Future

Founded in 2023, Eyi'hle Fuel & Energy has rapidly emerged as a powerhouse in the South African energy sector. Headquartered in Meyerton, we operate as a 100% black woman-owned entity, driven by a commitment to excellence and transformative leadership.

Our Level 1 B-BBEE credentials reflect our dedication to South Africa's economic development. We combine professional expertise with a robust supply chain to ensure seamless distribution of essential energy products to our diverse clientele.
